Project overview

The yaw damper improves the natural flying qualities of an aircraft, by increasing the damping coefficient of the Dutch roll through action on the rudder. Studies have shown that pilots are used to controlling systems with natural pulsations of less than 0.5 rad/s and damping of 0.5 or more. Aircraft whose dynamic behavior does not meet these criteria are considered to be tiring to fly, which is undesirable. So we're looking for a yaw damper that meets these specifications.
